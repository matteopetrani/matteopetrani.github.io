This site uses cookies and similar technologies to collect analytics data about how it is visited.

## Data controller

Matteo Petrani — [matteo.petrani@gmail.com](mailto:matteo.petrani@gmail.com)

## Tools used

**PostHog** — an analytics and UX tool. Data is stored on European servers (EU). PostHog acts as a data processor under Art. 28 GDPR, with a Data Processing Agreement (DPA) in place.

## Legal basis

Processing is based on your **consent** (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR), expressed via the banner on your first visit. You can withdraw it at any time.

## What is collected

- Pages visited and visit duration
- Clicks on links and interactive elements
- Device type, browser, and language
- UTM parameters (if you arrive from a campaign or newsletter)
- Scroll depth and reading time on posts
- Session recordings: anonymous sequences of page interactions (clicks, scrolls, cursor movements), used to improve the site experience. Text fields (e.g. the newsletter email input) are not recorded.
- JavaScript errors: unhandled technical exceptions, used to identify malfunctions
- Anonymized IP addresses

No data that allows direct identification of a person is intentionally collected.

## Data retention

| Data type | Duration |
|---|---|
| Session recordings | 30 days |
| Analytics data (events, pageviews) | 1 year |
| Consent preference | Stored locally in the browser, no expiry |

## Your rights

Under the GDPR you have the right to:

- **Access** the data held about you
- **Rectify** inaccurate data
- **Erase** your data ("right to be forgotten")
- **Restrict** processing
- **Object** to processing
- **Data portability**
- **Withdraw consent** at any time, without affecting the lawfulness of processing carried out before the withdrawal

To exercise your rights, contact me at [matteo.petrani@gmail.com](mailto:matteo.petrani@gmail.com). You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority. In Italy: [Garante per la protezione dei dati personali](https://www.garanteprivacy.it).

## How to disable tracking

Use the banner on your first visit to accept or decline. You can change your preference at any time by clearing the site's local data from your browser, or by contacting me directly.
